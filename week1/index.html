What is StudyFlow AI?

StudyFlow AI is a startup concept designed for students who want to study more efficiently. The platform uses artificial intelligence to summarise lecture notes, generate practice questions, and create personalised study plans.

The goal of the startup is to reduce study stress and help students stay organised during busy semesters.
